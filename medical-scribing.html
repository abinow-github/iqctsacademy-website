What Is Medical Scribing?

Medical scribing is the process of documenting medical information during patient visits. The scribe works closely with the healthcare provider to accurately record patient information, including medical history, symptoms, diagnoses, treatment plans, and medications. Medical scribing plays a vital role in improving patient care by allowing healthcare providers to spend more time with patients and less time on documentation.

In Kerala, the average salary for a medical scriber is around Rs. 2.5–3.5 lakhs per annum, depending on the level of experience and skills. The demand for medical scribing jobs in Kerala is growing due to the increasing number of healthcare facilities and hospitals in the city.
