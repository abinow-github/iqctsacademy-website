Medical Records Management

What is Medical Records Management?

Medical records management is the systematic and organised process of collecting, storing, organising, and maintaining patient-related information in healthcare facilities. These records encompass a comprehensive range of data, including medical history, diagnoses, treatment plans, test results, and other essential patient details. Effective medical records management ensures that healthcare providers can access accurate information promptly, enabling informed decision-making and continuity of care. Electronic health records (EHR) are central in the digital age, providing secure ways to store and exchange medical information. Proper record management enhances patient safety, care coordination, and compliance with data privacy and security regulations in healthcare.
